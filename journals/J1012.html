1.1: Describe the general process for executing addition, regardless of base.
You have to add the ones place then carry over any extra numbers. Then you repeat this step for all digits.
2.1: What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with octal numbers?
When adding octal numbers you have to carry over when you reach 8 instead of 10 because you only have 8 different digits.
3.1: What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with hexadecimal numbers?
You have to carry over in hexadecimal when you reach a 16. This is because hex has 16 digits instead of 10. Also you have to remember to include letter for digits greater than 9.
4.1: What special factors need to be considered when executing addition with binary numbers?
You have to remeber that it only has two different digits so if you get a two you have to carry that number over to a new spot.
